Biography

A lecturer and researcher in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at United International University, Bangladesh. Completed Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Engineering as the valedictorian with a perfect CGPA of 4.00 and currently pursuing Master's degree in the same field.

Research interests are focused on applied machine learning, with particular emphasis on computer vision, natural language processing, algorithm design, and reinforcement learning.
